THE LAWS OF THE TREASURY

01

The Solitary Kanon Clause

The Treasurer is permitted to consume Grolsch Kanon in solitude, provided it is consumed directly from a cold 0.5L can and logged in the system within 2 hours of opening.

02

The Toast of the Ledger

Every bottle of Ketel 1 opened by the Treasurer must be inaugurated with a communal toast to the board budget. The auditor present is legally obliged to double-check that the seal is broken and the event is recorded.

03

Auditor Immunity

Logged items can only be approved and submitted by designated Auditors (board members with access to the shared secret). The Treasurer is prohibited from self-logging without witness verification.

04

The Sovereign Balance

Should the Unopened Debt exceed €150.00, the Treasurer's voting rights on financial decisions are suspended until a bank transfer/cash deposit is recorded, reducing the debt to acceptable operational levels.

ABOUT THE SCOREBOARD

Treasurer's Bottle Scoreboard

This system was custom-built for the Study Association as an open, auditable record of the Treasurer's heavy-caliber consumption. Designed around the iconic Grolsch Kanon (11.6% ABV) cans and Ketel 1 Jonge Graanjenever bottles, the scoreboard acts as a ledger to calculate beer debt and maintain financial integrity.

Technical Stack

Built with native HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, powered by Vercel Serverless API functions and Vercel KV for global state management. Secures edits via HMAC SHA-256 session signatures.
